Bathala Coin (BTHLA) — Whitepaper

1. Introduction

Bathala Coin (BTHLA) is a mythology-powered memecoin born from the idea that even the ancient gods would’ve loved a good pump. Inspired by Bathala — the OG creator of the universe — we bring Filipino folklore into the modern digital arena through memes, hype, and community energy.

BTHLA isn’t here to lecture with long mythology lessons. Instead, we turn Philippine mythological icons into fun, iconic, shareable moments that anyone around the world can vibe with.

Think of it as cultural storytelling compressed into:

  • Memes
  • Art
  • Characters
  • Lore drops
  • Community jokes

We don’t force people to learn mythology — we make them want to, because the imagery is cool, the memes are fire, and the vibe is legendary.

2. Cultural Mission

BTHLA was created with one core mission:

Make Philippine mythology cool again.

Not through lectures. Not through academic presentations. But through community energy, storytelling, and meme culture that fits the new generation.

We aim to:

  • Celebrate Filipino identity in a fun, accessible way
  • Reintroduce mythological characters through memes and digital art
  • Inspire pride and creativity
  • Showcase Filipino folklore globally
  • Make mythology enjoyable without requiring formal study

We’re not trying to be historians — we’re here to hype our heritage in the most iconic way possible.
